ABOUT


The GEMINI ANGELS band was founded by three best college bestfriends namely, Jessica, Dom and Bailey. Their love for music brought them together when they enrolled in a music class in their sophomore year of college.







The Story

Jessica, Dominique (Dom), and Bailey first met in 2017 when they crossed paths at the University of South Carolina — Upstate in Spartanburg, SC. Jessica and Bailey were roommates on campus at USC Upstate, then they met Dom, who was their Resident Assistant (RA) for the year. They clicked instantly and ever since then, they have been the best of friends.

Soon after they met, they began to bond over music and contemplated the idea of starting a band together. They value women’s empowerment, so they wanted to make music that would inspire and impact girls of all ages. So…they did just that.

Band name Meaning

As their fanbase has grown over the years, they have gotten many questions regarding the name of their band. So, why “Gemini Angels?” All three of them were born in early June, which means their zodiac signs are all gemini. They felt as though that similarity was unique, so they decided to utilize that characteristic between them. Then…the “angels” part of their name was just totally random!
